<h3><strong>MOE Financial Assistance 2026</strong></h3>
<p><strong><u>MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS)</u></strong>
<br>Download the form and submit the completed form with all supporting documents
physically at the school’s general office. Download the form&nbsp;<a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1i8PeFTRsKhfJiWVlYsm43jRDg7ZGUpya/view?usp=sharing" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.
<br>
<br>Primary 6 pupils can apply for MOE FAS for Secondary 1 by submitting the
application to the Primary School <u>by 17 Dec 2025</u>. Their primary schools
will process the applications and inform them of the outcome.</p>
<p>If you missed the deadline, you may apply with your new secondary school.
This is<u> </u>to facilitate smooth and timely collection of books and
uniforms before the new academic year starts.</p>
<p>For more information, you may contact the school’s general office or refer
to MOE’s website (<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance</a>).
<br>
<br><strong><u>Collection of FAS Items</u></strong>
<br>Students who are successful in their FAS application (approved by primary
school) can visit the school during office hours from 19 December 2025
onwards.
<br>No appointment is needed.
<br>Please proceed to the General Office with your FAS approval letter and
Student EZ-Link card from primary school.
<br>The FAS staff-in-charge will issue another FAS approval letter on the
Juying Secondary School letterhead to present to the bookshop and uniform
vendor.
<br>
<br>The staff-in-charge will also issue to you the following items:
<br>Ø&nbsp; <strong>Secondary 1 Booklist</strong>
<br>Ø&nbsp; <strong>Uniform Sizing list</strong>
<br>Ø&nbsp; <strong>Queue number</strong>
</p>
<p>Please ensure that you indicate your class and stream on the booklist
and tick the required books accurately so that the bookshop can pack them
correctly.</p>
<p><strong>Please double-check the books and other items received under FAS before you exit the school.</strong>
</p>
