<h3><strong>MOE Financial Assistance 2026</strong></h3>
<p><strong><u>MOE Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE FAS)</u></strong>
<br>Primary 6 pupils can continue to apply for MOE FAS for Secondary 1 by
submitting the application to their respective primary schools by 17 Dec
2025. Their primary schools will process the applications and inform them
of the outcome.</p>
<p>You may also apply to Juying Secondary School upon receiving your posting
results on
<br><strong>19 December 2025</strong>. Please obtain a hard copy of the application
form from the General Office or download the soft copy <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/17TynGMoySIg_S3v0YNFJKRNmSnFHWfpB/view?usp=sharing" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>Kindly complete the application form and prepare all required supporting
documents. Please submit your application by <strong>24 December 2025</strong> so
that you will be able to collect your FAS items before the school reopens.</p>
<p>For more information, you may contact the school’s general office or refer
to MOE’s website (<a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance</a>).</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong><u>Collection of FAS Items</u></strong>
<br>Students who are successful in their FAS application (approved by primary
school) can visit the school during office hours from 19 December 2025
onwards. <strong>Please bring along the FAS approval letter from primary school.</strong>
<br>
<br>No appointment is needed.</p>
<p>Please proceed to the General Office with your FAS approval letter and
Student EZ-Link card from primary school.</p>
<p>The FAS staff-in-charge will issue another FAS approval letter on the
Juying Secondary School letterhead to present to the bookshop and uniform
vendor.</p>
<p>The staff-in-charge will also issue to you the following items:
<br>Ø&nbsp; <strong>Secondary 1 Booklist</strong>
<br>Ø&nbsp; <strong>Uniform Sizing list</strong>
<br>Ø&nbsp; <strong>Queue number</strong>
</p>
<p>Please ensure that you indicate your class and stream on the booklist
and tick the required books accurately so that the bookshop can pack them
correctly.</p>
<p><strong>Please double-check the books and other items received under FAS before you exit the school.</strong>
</p>
