<h4><strong>Vision</strong></h4>
<p>Inculcating values through the love of arts.</p>
<h4><strong>Mission</strong></h4>
<p>A platform to inculcate interest and strengthen character through Cultural
Dance.</p>
<h4><strong>Overview</strong></h4>
<p>Cultural Dance is a captivating expression of the values, traditions,
and identity of the JYian community. Cultural dance embodies a rich tapestry
of stories, beliefs, and experiences of the multi-racial hotpot that is
Juying.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This would not be possible without the care and guidance from our CCA
teachers-in-charge, Ms Suhana, Ms Goh Wan Ru and Ms Yeo Yee Teng as well
as our esteemed instructor, Mr Quek Jiang Da who has helped students learn
dance steps while teaching them about culture and values.</p>
<p>In Cultural Dance, the students not only develop and strengthen their
understanding of Chinese Contemporary dance skills, but they also learn
and appreciate other ethnic dance elements, such as Malay Dance and also
Modern Dance. Furthermore, dance serves as a powerful medium for imparting
important values such as resilience, tenacity, and adaptability to the
students.&nbsp;</p>
<p>It is also a tradition for Cultural Dance to perform for the National
Day Celebration in school. This year’s performance will yet again be another
breakthrough, with the dancers attempting a relatively new genre; modern
dance.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our Cultural Dance bonding camp held annually every November encourages
our students to learn more about each other so as to respect their peers’
diversity in races and religion. It also provided ample opportunities for
students to display their graciousness by looking out for others and embracing
teamwork by sharing with one another what they know about dance.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Thus, our CCA is definitely an established platform to support and develop
our members in Juying Secondary School on character building and aesthetic
talents.</p>
